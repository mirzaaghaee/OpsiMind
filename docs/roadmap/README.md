# 🗺️ Opsimind Development Roadmap

> **Building the next generation AI Operations Intelligence Platform — one foundation at a time.**

---

## Overview

Welcome to the **Opsimind Development Roadmap**.

This roadmap documents the engineering journey of **Opsimind V1**, describing **what** will be built, **why** it is being built, and **how** the platform will evolve over the coming year.

Unlike traditional product roadmaps that primarily list features and release dates, the Opsimind roadmap focuses on the **engineering evolution** of the platform. Every phase is designed to establish the capabilities required for the next one, resulting in a production-grade AI Operations Intelligence Platform.

This documentation is intended to provide complete transparency into the project's architecture, priorities, and implementation strategy.

---

# Why a Roadmap?

Opsimind is not being developed as a collection of disconnected features.

Instead, it is being engineered as a layered platform where each capability builds upon a solid foundation.

Rather than rushing toward AI functionality, the project prioritizes long-term architecture, maintainability, scalability, and production readiness.

This roadmap serves as both:

* a public engineering plan for contributors and followers;
* a reference guide for future development decisions;
* a progress tracker throughout the V1 journey;
* a living document that evolves alongside the platform.

---

# Development Philosophy

Opsimind follows several core engineering principles throughout its development.

* **Architecture before implementation**
* **Documentation as a first-class deliverable**
* **Cloud-native by design**
* **AI built on reliable engineering foundations**
* **Production-grade quality from the beginning**
* **Incremental and measurable progress**
* **Continuous learning and refinement**

Every major design decision should support these principles.

# V1 Development Strategy

The first release of Opsimind is divided into four major phases.

Each phase establishes the capabilities required for the next stage of the platform.

```text
Engineering Foundation
        │
        ▼
Data Foundation
        │
        ▼
Intelligence Foundation
        │
        ▼
Enterprise Platform
```

This progression ensures that intelligence is built upon reliable infrastructure rather than being added as an isolated feature.

---

# Development Phases

## Phase 1 — Engineering Foundation

The Engineering Foundation establishes the core platform architecture.

This phase focuses on software engineering excellence, cloud-native infrastructure, development workflows, platform services, security, testing, deployment automation, and operational readiness.

**Goal**

Build a robust, scalable, and production-ready platform capable of supporting future intelligence capabilities.

---

## Phase 2 — Data Foundation

The Data Foundation enables the platform to collect, process, organize, and manage operational information.

This phase introduces event ingestion, telemetry pipelines, storage, streaming, operational data models, and knowledge management capabilities.

**Goal**

Transform raw operational data into trusted information assets that can power intelligent analysis.

---

## Phase 3 — Intelligence Foundation

The Intelligence Foundation transforms data into actionable operational knowledge.

This phase introduces AI services, reasoning engines, prediction models, knowledge graphs, root cause analysis, recommendations, and autonomous decision support.

**Goal**

Enable Opsimind to understand, reason about, and improve complex operational systems.

---

## Phase 4 — Enterprise Platform

The final phase prepares Opsimind for enterprise-scale adoption.

It includes multi-tenancy, security hardening, governance, scalability, enterprise integrations, operational resilience, and production deployment capabilities.

**Goal**

Deliver an enterprise-ready AI Operations Intelligence Platform suitable for real-world production environments.
