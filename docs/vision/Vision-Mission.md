> **Version:** 3.0

# Vision

**To become the world's most trusted Operational Decision Intelligence Platform, empowering engineering teams to make faster, safer, and smarter operational decisions across complex distributed systems.**

---

# Mission

**Build an open, cloud-native platform that transforms operational telemetry, historical knowledge, and engineering experience into trusted operational decisions through operational reasoning, analytics, prediction, simulation, optimization, explainable AI, and progressive autonomy.**

---

# Category

## Operational Decision Intelligence

OpsiMind defines a new category:

> **Operational Decision Intelligence (ODI)**

Traditional observability platforms focus on understanding the past.

**Observability explains what happened.**

**OpsiMind helps decide what should happen next.**

Operational Decision Intelligence combines operational telemetry, engineering knowledge, reasoning, predictive analytics, simulation, and explainable AI to support trusted operational decisions across modern distributed systems.
