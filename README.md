## Overview

Opsimind is an open engineering project focused on building the next generation of enterprise operations intelligence.

Modern enterprises generate enormous volumes of operational data—logs, metrics, traces, events, topology information, deployment history, and runtime behavior. Existing observability platforms help collect and visualize this data, but understanding still depends heavily on human expertise.

Opsimind aims to transform operational data into operational intelligence.

Instead of simply reporting what happened, Opsimind is designed to help engineers understand why systems behave the way they do, anticipate future behavior, recommend actions, and progressively automate operational decision-making.

---

## Vision

To become the cognitive layer of enterprise operations.

---

## Mission

Enable enterprise systems to understand themselves, learn from experience, and continuously improve operational decision-making.

---

## Philosophy

We believe operational excellence begins with understanding.

Monitoring tells us what happened.

Observability explains why.

Operational intelligence enables better decisions.

Opsimind exists to bridge that gap.

---

## Long-Term Goals

Opsimind is being designed to evolve through several stages.

```
Observe
      ↓
Understand
      ↓
Learn
      ↓
Reason
      ↓
Predict
      ↓
Recommend
      ↓
Optimize
      ↓
Automate
```

Each stage builds upon the previous one, allowing enterprises to progressively move from reactive operations toward intelligent and eventually autonomous operations.
